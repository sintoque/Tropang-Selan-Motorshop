About Us

The story behind Tropang Selan

Who We Are

Tropang Selan is a motorcycle shop established in 2023 by Hervert Andres and Irene S. Cruz. The business operates from its local location to serve motorcycle owners who are aged between 18 and 50.

We offer complete access to motorcycle parts and accessories along with repair, customization, and maintenance services. Tropang Selan provides solutions for all motorcycle problems which especially includes engine repairs.

Tropang Selan currently operates its physical store but intends to create online platforms to help serve more customers while simplifying their inquiry and booking process.

Our Vision

Tropang Selan aims to expand its customer base while building a strong online presence — making motorcycle ownership easier and more enjoyable for all city riders.
